Join the movement
We are building a movement committed to making data a tool for social change instead of a weapon of political oppression in the lives of Black people.

This movement is multigenerational and multiracial. It includes scientists, technologists, educators, designers, policy makers, organizers, parents, and students.
For us, this is about more than just data. This is about fighting new forms of racism and discrimination enabled by technology and unleashing the true potential of data to uplift and empower communities. Join our movement!
